Approach

I base my tutoring on the individual's need, capability and previous learning or knowledge. Where prior learning or basics are missing, I patch in these missing pieces as the tutoring progresses so as to have the student acquire the prerequisite basic knowledge while covering the curriculum. This is reinforced with regular 'build up' tests and revisions.
